## üåü User Guide
Once you've installed InvoiceFlow, you can start using it right away. Here's a brief guide to help you navigate the application:

1. **Create Your Account:** Open InvoiceFlow and create a new account using your email address.
2. **Add Products:** Go to the inventory section and enter your products with their prices and descriptions.
3. **Create an Invoice:** Navigate to the invoice section, select your customer, and choose the products sold.
4. **Send Invoices:** Once your invoice is ready, you can email it directly to your customers from within the app.
5. **Track Payments:** Mark invoices as paid once you receive payments to keep your records updated.
